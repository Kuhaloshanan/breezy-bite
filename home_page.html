About us

Breezy bite bakery is rooted in 40 years ago near Kandy city. Has grown from modest beginning to a renowned establishment. Originating as a small bakery specializing in essentials like bread and buns. Our initial focus on affordability attracted local resident people and students, propelling us to popularity. Bakery success paved the way for expansion and as we opened more shops, we eventually transformed into a sizable bakery. By 1995 we proudly operated seven thriving businesses, each contributing to our growing reputation. To meet the diverse tastes of our expanding customer base, we diversified oue menu with an array of culinary delights. In the year 2000 we encountered a new challenge in the form of competition from industry giants like KFC, Pizza hut and burger king. Instead of succumbing to the pressure we embraced the opportunity to venture into fast food, a decision that proved successful. Today our menu boasts a wide variety of food, show casing our adaptability and commitment to satisfying diverse plates. In recognition of the evolving landscape of consumer habits especially the prevalence of technology we are actively working on a website. This digital platform aims to streamline the online ordering process, catering the preference of our customers, particularly the younger generation who frequently use smartphones and internet. Our goal is to expand our reach, attract more customers and continue growing our business.
